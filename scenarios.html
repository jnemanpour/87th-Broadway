90003 is a USDA food desert, HRSA medically underserved area, and childcare desert. These designations unlock additional funding (NMTC, HFFI, CDFI) and ensure guaranteed tenant demand for ground-floor uses.

Recommendation: Scenario 4 — Phase It

Why this is the best play for long-term cash flow:
  • Lowest cash in ($7.3M total) — Phase 1 developer fee ($5M) funds most of Phase 2 equity
  • Highest Phase 1 cash-on-cash (9.1%) — proves concept to lenders and investors
  • De-risks everything — you build on vacant land first (no demo, no existing tenants, no environmental)
  • 90-day entitlement via SB 35 — Phase 1 can break ground in 12 months (your timeline)
  • Manchester Urban Homes next door is literally leasing up right now — social proof for your project
  • Phase 2 mixed-use village is where the vision lives — grocery, clinic, daycare — but you build it from a position of strength with cash flow behind you
  • Combined $2.9M NOI / $104K monthly cash flow at stabilization — same as Scenario 1 but with 60% less cash out of pocket
