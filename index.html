Risks & Due Diligence

15-day contingency is dangerously short for 15 industrial parcels in Vernon. Push for 60-90 days minimum. Phase I ESA alone takes 2-4 weeks.

Environmental HIGH RISK

  • Vernon has extensive industrial contamination history
  • Exide Technologies Superfund site nearby — lead/arsenic
  • 25+ sites with leaking underground storage tanks
  • "As Is" sale = you inherit whatever is in the ground
  • Action: Phase I ESA on ALL 15 parcels immediately
  • Action: Check DTSC EnviroStor + GeoTracker for each APN
  • Budget: $27K-$69K for Phase I; $15K-$50K for Phase II if triggered

Dual Agency MEDIUM RISK

  • Lee & Associates represents BOTH buyer and seller
  • Broker cannot advocate for lowest price while pursuing highest
  • Your negotiation strategy is exposed to the seller's agent
  • Action: Retain your own independent real estate attorney immediately
  • Action: Request in writing what Lee has disclosed to seller about you

Multi-Parcel Complexity MEDIUM RISK

  • 15 separate parcels each need title search, environmental review
  • Each may carry different easements, encumbrances
  • Trust-held property requires verification of trustee authority
  • APN typo possible: "6040-0150016" may be "6040-015-016"; "6040-104-007" may be "6040-014-007"
  • Action: Preliminary title report on all 15 parcels

Building Condition MEDIUM RISK

  • $113/SF building price suggests poor condition or obsolescence
  • Possible unreinforced masonry (seismic risk)
  • Asbestos/lead paint likely in pre-1980 buildings
  • Action: Structural/seismic assessment, roof inspection, hazmat survey
