## 1. INTERACTIVE PARCEL & ZONING MAPS

### LA County Assessor -- Parcel Map Viewer
- **URL:** https://portal.assessor.lacounty.gov/mapsearch
- **How to use:** Click "Search by APN" and enter your parcel number (e.g., 6040-019-001). The map will zoom to the parcel, show boundaries, and display assessed values. You can toggle aerial imagery and building footprints. Print Assessor maps directly from the interface.
- **Also try:** The PAIS system at https://maps.assessor.lacounty.gov for mobile-friendly parcel viewing with aerial overlays.

### ZIMAS (Zone Information & Map Access System) -- LA City Zoning
- **URL:** https://zimas.lacity.org/
- **How to use:** Enter "8701 S Broadway" in the address search. ZIMAS will show zoning designation, General Plan land use, Community Plan Area (South Los Angeles or Southeast Los Angeles), specific plan overlays, and case history. Select "Planning and Zoning" from the dropdown, then check the "Zone" field. Click on the zone to see permitted uses and development standards.
- **Note:** ZIMAS covers LA City only. For Vernon parcels, use the Vernon tools below.
- **Tutorial:** https://planning.lacity.gov/blog/how-does-zimas-work

### City of Vernon -- Interactive GIS Map
- **URL:** https://experience.arcgis.com/experience/bddf8ea4f08440d68744eb042752ed7c
- **How to use:** This ArcGIS-based viewer shows Vernon parcel boundaries, zoning districts, and infrastructure. Search by address or pan to the western Vernon area near Alameda Street to view your target parcels. Layer controls let you toggle zoning, parcels, and aerial imagery.
- **City Maps page:** https://www.cityofvernonca.gov/government/public-works-division/city-maps

### LA County Enterprise GIS Portal
- **URL:** https://egis-lacounty.hub.arcgis.com/
- **How to use:** This is the master GIS hub for all LA County spatial data. Search for datasets like "parcels," "zoning," "land use," or "Service Planning Areas." You can view data layers online or download shapefiles for your own GIS analysis. Useful for overlaying multiple data layers across both LA City and Vernon.
- **Parcel data specifically:** https://hub.arcgis.com/datasets/lacounty::la-county-parcel-map-service/about

### Google Maps -- Pre-Centered Links
- **8701 S Broadway:** https://www.google.com/maps/place/8701+S+Broadway,+Los+Angeles,+CA+90003
- **Vernon Industrial Area (centered on Alameda & Vernon Ave):** https://www.google.com/maps/@33.9947,-118.2307,15z
- **How to use:** Switch to Satellite view for aerial context. Use Street View to assess building conditions, street frontage, and surrounding uses. Measure distances between your owned parcel and target Vernon parcels using the ruler tool (right-click > Measure distance).

## 2. OPPORTUNITY ZONE MAPS

### HUD Opportunity Zones Map
- **URL:** https://opportunityzones.hud.gov/resources/map
- **How to use:** Enter "8701 S Broadway, Los Angeles" or a Vernon address in the search bar. The map will highlight designated Qualified Opportunity Zones (QOZs) in blue. OZ 1.0 designations are in effect through end of 2028. In 2026, governors will nominate new tracts for OZ 2.0 (effective through 2036). Check whether each of your target parcels falls within a designated QOZ census tract.

### California State Integrated OZ Map
- **URL:** https://opzones.ca.gov/oz-map/
- **How to use:** This state-specific map overlays OZ boundaries with California state investments (Caltrans projects, ARB low-carbon projects, High Speed Rail). Useful for identifying where state capital is flowing near your parcels and whether state incentive programs apply.

### CDFI Fund CIMS Mapping Tool
- **URL:** https://www.cdfifund.gov/mapping-system
- **How to use:** Enter each property address to determine eligibility for CDFI Fund programs including New Markets Tax Credits (NMTC), Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFI) program, and other distress-based criteria. Click "Layers" tab and select "Opportunity Zone" to see QOZ boundaries in blue.
- **Also:** https://www.cdfifund.gov/opportunity-zones for OZ-specific resources.

### CTCAC/HCD Opportunity Area Maps (California Treasurer)
- **URL:** https://www.treasurer.ca.gov/ctcac/opportunity.asp
- **How to use:** These maps show TCAC/HCD Opportunity Areas for tax credit purposes. Download the 2026 Opportunity Maps, methodology docs, and shapefiles. Relevant if any residential or mixed-use component is planned for Vernon parcels.

### Novogradac OZ Mapping Tool
- **URL:** https://www.novoco.com/resource-centers/opportunity-zones-resource-center/novogradac-opportunity-zones-mapping-tool
- **How to use:** Enter addresses to verify OZ designation. This tool also shows areas eligible for OZ 2.0 designation. Useful for cross-referencing HUD and CDFI maps.

## 3. ENVIRONMENTAL MAPS

### DTSC EnviroStor (Hazardous Waste & Cleanup Sites)
- **URL:** https://www.envirostor.dtsc.ca.gov/public/
- **How to use:** Navigate to the Vernon/South LA area or enter addresses in the search bar. The map shows cleanup sites, permitted hazardous waste facilities, and investigation sites with color-coded markers by status. Click any marker for site details, documents, and cleanup status. CRITICAL for due diligence on Vernon industrial parcels -- check each target APN for active or historical contamination cases.

### State Water Board GeoTracker (Groundwater & Soil Contamination)
- **URL:** https://geotracker.waterboards.ca.gov/
- **How to use:** Search by address or zoom to the 87th/Broadway and Vernon area. GeoTracker maps Leaking Underground Storage Tank (LUST) sites, Department of Defense sites, and cleanup program sites. Toggle between street, satellite, and terrain views. Click individual sites for monitoring data, reports, and case status. Vernon's industrial history means many LUST and cleanup sites are in the area -- review each carefully before acquisition.

### CalEnviroScreen 4.0 (Cumulative Environmental Burden)
- **URL (Interactive Dashboard):** https://experience.arcgis.com/experience/6b863505f9454cea802f4be0b4b49d62
- **URL (Main page):** https://oehha.ca.gov/calenviroscreen/maps-data
- **URL (ArcGIS Hub):** https://calenviroscreen-oehha.hub.arcgis.com/
- **How to use:** Search by address or census tract. CalEnviroScreen scores every census tract in California using 21 indicators (pollution burden + population characteristics). Higher scores = greater cumulative burden. The South LA/Vernon area scores high, which can support applications for environmental justice funding, grants, and regulatory flexibility. Filter by individual indicators (e.g., hazardous waste, groundwater threats, cleanup sites) to understand specific risks.
- **Note:** Draft CalEnviroScreen 5.0 was released January 2026 -- check for updates.

### EPA EJScreen (Environmental Justice Screening)
- **Original URL:** https://ejscreen.epa.gov/mapper/ (currently offline as of February 2025)
- **Reconstructed version:** https://screening-tools.com/epa-ejscreen
- **Alternative reconstruction:** https://pedp-ejscreen.azurewebsites.net/
- **How to use:** Enter an address or draw a boundary around your target area. EJScreen combines 12 environmental indicators with 6 demographic indicators to produce EJ indexes. Useful for grant applications requiring environmental justice documentation. The reconstructed versions maintain Version 2.3 data.

## 5. TRANSIT & INFRASTRUCTURE MAPS

### LA Metro System Map

### Broadway-Manchester Active Transportation (ATP) Equity Project
- **Official project page:** https://engineering.lacity.gov/about-us/divisions/environmental-management/projects/b-m-atp
- **Interactive StoryMap:** https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/3f52b65d816b4d8da963033c2c2ece1c

Your property at 8701 S Broadway is within the project corridor. Construction is phased through 2026. The StoryMap provides detailed maps of each improvement phase.

### Alameda Corridor Map
- **URL (Union Pacific PDF map):** https://www.up.com/cs/groups/public/documents/up_pdf_nativedocs/pdf_alameda_up_maps.pdf
- **ACTA (Alameda Corridor Transportation Authority):** https://www.acta.org/about/

The mid-corridor trench runs along Alameda Street -- directly adjacent to Vernon's western boundary and your target parcels. The trench emerges at 25th Street near Vernon.
