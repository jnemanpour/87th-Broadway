# Commercial Property Comparable Sales
## 87th & Broadway, Los Angeles CA 90003 — South LA / Broadway-Manchester Corridor
### Research Date: March 26, 2026

---

## SUMMARY OF FINDINGS

The South Broadway corridor (roughly 59th St to Imperial Hwy) in South LA remains an undervalued but active commercial market. Key takeaways:

- **Vacant C2-zoned land** along S Broadway trades in the range of **$30-$95/SF** depending on lot size, contamination, TOC eligibility, and frontage
- **Improved commercial/retail buildings** along S Broadway are assessed or listed at **$267-$552/SF** of building area
- **Development sites** with entitlements or TOC potential command significant premiums
- **$85M Broadway-Manchester Complete Streets project** (construction began late 2024, completion ~late 2026) is a major catalyst
- **Measure ULA** (4% tax on sales >$5M, 5.5% on >$10M) continues to dampen larger transactions
- The market is in a recovery phase after 2023-2024 softness, with 2025 LA metro CRE sales reaching $37.3B (up from $33B in 2024)

## SECTION 7: INFRASTRUCTURE & CATALYSTS AFFECTING VALUES

### Broadway-Manchester Complete Streets Project ($85M)
- **Scope:** 2.8 miles — Manchester Ave (Vermont to Broadway) + S Broadway (Manchester to Imperial)
- **Features:** Protected bike lanes, pedestrian refuge islands, curb extensions, street trees, pedestrian lighting, stormwater capture
- **Funding:** SCAG Active Transportation Program, Measure W
- **Timeline:** Construction began late 2024; phased completion through late 2026
- **Impact:** Directly bisects the 87th & Broadway project area

### Metro Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) — Vermont Corridor
- 8601 S Vermont acquisition positioned near planned BRT line
- $139M federal grant secured for Metro bus-only lanes on ~10-mile Broadway stretch (Chinatown to South LA) in advance of 2028 Olympics

### Broadway/Manchester CPIO (Community Plan Implementation Overlay)
- Tiered overlay affecting development standards along the corridor
- Tier 3 designation (most permissive) applies to several nearby parcels

### Transit Oriented Communities (TOC)
- Properties near Metro stops qualify for density bonuses
- Multiple Broadway corridor parcels show TOC eligibility for 20-40+ units
